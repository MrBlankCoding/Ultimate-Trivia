Privacy Policy for Ultimate Trivia

Effective Date: 9/12/24

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Ultimate Trivia ("we", "our", or "the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord trivia bot.

2. Information We Collect

The only personal information we collect is:

  • Discord User IDs: We collect and store Discord User IDs solely for the purpose of maintaining a leaderboard for our trivia games.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected Discord User IDs exclusively for:

  • Creating and updating the trivia game leaderboard
  • Associating your game scores with your User ID

We do not use this information for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • User IDs are stored securely on our servers.
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or accidental loss.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade User IDs with third parties.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data (User ID)
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to our use of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 9.

7. Data Retention

We retain User IDs for as long as necessary to maintain the leaderboard. If you stop using the Bot or request deletion of your data, we will remove your User ID from our database.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting an announcement in the Bot's support server.
